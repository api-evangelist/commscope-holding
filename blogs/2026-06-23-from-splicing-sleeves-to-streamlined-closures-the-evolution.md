This article traces the historical evolution of fiber splice protection technology, from early labor-intensive field splicing methods to modern CommScope FOSC closure systems. The piece highlights how standardized fiber closures have transformed field deployment by improving reliability, reducing installation complexity, and enabling faster network expansion while maintaining durability in harsh outdoor conditions.
