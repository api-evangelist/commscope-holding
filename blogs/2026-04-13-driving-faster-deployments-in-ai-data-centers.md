There are two main drivers fueling the current AI data center  dynamic growth (or space race), and those are providing more capacity and delivering  quicker go-live times. Both are mission critical to data centers dealing with fast-evolving GenAI deployments; AI is such a competitive space right now that better logistics and faster turn-up can be just as important as deploying the latest AI architecture.
