Broadband networks are still in the midst of the current multi-year cycle of expansion and upgrades, both in the U.S. and around the world. This isn’t going to slow down in 2025, but I do expect to see the approach to modulate to feature a preference for less complex and easily-handled solutions. Smaller and simpler will be the key priorities.
