CommScope (NASDAQ: COMM), a global leader in network connectivity, announced today that the infrastructure company Wyre, has selected CommScope’s cloud-native vCCAP Evo™ solution for Remote PHY (R-PHY) deployments as the foundation for their next-generation broadband access network in Belgium.
